INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy describes how Lunes Hosting ("Lunes," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and discloses your information when you use our services ("Services"). This Privacy Policy applies to all users of our Services, including visitors to our website, customers who purchase our Services, and users of our Services who have signed up for an account. By using our Services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

We collect information about you when you use our Services. This information includes the following:

Information You Provide to Us

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Usernam
  • Password
  • Billing address
  • IP address (for security purposes)

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device information
  • Search history (if not opted out)
  • Browsing history (if not opted out)

HOW DO WE USE YOUR INFORMATION?

We use the information to:
  • Provide, improve, and administer our Services
  • Respond to your inquiries and offer support
  • Send administrative information and communications
  • Fulfill and manage your orders
  • Secure our Services and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING YOUR INFORMATION

We process your information based on the following legal grounds:
  • Contract: We need your information to fulfill our contractual obligations to you.
  • Consent: You can withdraw your consent at any time.
  • Legitimate obligations: We may need to disclose your information to comply with the law.
  • Vital Interest: We may use your information to protect your vital interests or the vital interests of others. (e.g., preventing harm)

WHEN AND WITH WHOM DO WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION?

We may share your information with the following parties:
  • Business partners (for offering specific products or services)
  • Service providers (who help us operate our Services)
  • Law enforcement or regulatory bodies (as required by law)

YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your information, including:
  • Access and obtain a copy of your information
  • Request rectification or erasure of your information
  • Restrict processing of your information
  • Object to processing of your information
  • Data portability (where applicable)

CALIFORNIA RESIDENTS

California residents have specific privacy rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). You can learn more about these rights and exercise them in the "California Residents" section below.

SECURITY

We take appropriate measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

UPDATES TO THIS NOTICE

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. We recommend that you review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

CONTACT US

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@lunes.host.

HOW TO ACCESS, UPDATE, OR DELETE YOUR DATA

You can submit a request to access, update, or delete your information by contacting us at support@lunes.host. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.
