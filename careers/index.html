At Lunes, we offer opportunities for entry-level positions in various fields, including computer engineering, business development, marketing, and programming. We're also flexible and open to part-time, full-time, or contract arrangements.

While these positions are currently volunteer-based, we are committed to compensating our team members competitively in the future.

If you are interested in strengthening your resume or gathering the experience of working at a cloud hosting company then feel free to reach out via our Discord.

RESPONSIBILITIES
  • Responds to Support Tickets on the Discord platform
REQUIREMENTS
  • Basic knowledge of Discord bots
  • Patience, respectful communication skills
BONUS REQUIREMENTS
  • Experience with NodeJS
IT ENGINEER
Provides server troubleshooting for the community, helps users with bots, and sometimes assists Project Managers with technical tasks.
RESPONSIBILITIES
  • Help with individual's problems
  • Assists Project Managers with technical tasks
REQUIREMENTS
  • Basic knowledge of Discord bots
  • Patience, respectful communication skills
BONUS REQUIREMENTS
  • Experience with NodeJS
PROJECT MANAGER
Plans and assigns project tasks, collaborates with IT Engineers to ensure on-time completion.
RESPONSIBILITIES
  • Plans and assigns project tasks
  • Collaborates with IT Engineers
  • Sends progress updates to either the Manager or CEO
REQUIREMENTS
  • Basic knowledge of Discord bots
  • Patience, strong and respectful communication skills
  • Strong leadership skills
  • Time Management
BONUS REQUIREMENTS
  • Experience with management
